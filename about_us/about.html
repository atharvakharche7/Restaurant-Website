About Us

Born in Mumbai in 2003, we’ve grown to only 1 beautiful restaurant across the India with more than 100 family members, many of whom have been here since day one.

Bombay Cuisines is all about making the most out of life: celebrating life’s little moments, making every day extraordinary, and making the everyday exquisite.

Our Menus


Our contemporary Italian menu takes fresh, carefully selected, simple ingredients to create the best tasting food and drink around.

Our Restaurants


Our timeless, classic restaurant make any occasion special – whether it’s date night, a family lunch or a catch up over cocktails – you’ll have an experience you’ll never forget under our olive trees.

Our History


Bombay Cuisines was founded in 2003 by Atharva Kharche, from Nagpur. In 2014, we gained private equity backing from Palatine.

We continue to do what we’ve done since day one: Make A Difference, Do The Right Thing, Look For The Good, and Share Our Passion & Knowledge.
